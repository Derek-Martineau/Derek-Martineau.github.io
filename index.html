Timeline

May 2023
I was selected as a recipient of the 2023 Robert E. Briney Computer Science Service Award.

Dr. Robert E. Briney was a member of the Salem State College faculty from 1968 through his retirement in 2008. He was a faculty member of the Mathematics Department until 1977 prior to becoming the chair of Salem State’s Computer Science (CS) department. He then served as the chair of the CS department for four terms. He was the principal author of the proposal that led to the establishment of a Computer Science major in 1984. He made significant contributions to the major’s curriculum development and ABET accreditation up to his retirement. To honor the contributions made by Dr. Briney, the Robert E. Briney Foundation established this award in 2021.

The Robert E. Briney Computer Science Service Award is awarded each academic year to the undergraduate junior/senior student in the Computer Science major program who served the major and the department in general. Through your hard work and determination, you have proven to be a student that the CS Department wants to recognize.
